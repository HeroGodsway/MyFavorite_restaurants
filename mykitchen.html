Jollof Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 4 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 cups of chicken or vegetable broth

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and minced garlic. Saut√© until the onions are translucent.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and bell peppers. Cook until the vegetables are soft and well combined.
  4. Stir in the thyme, curry powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, and salt. Cook for a few minutes to enhance the flavors.
  5. Add the rice to the pot and stir to coat it evenly with the tomato and spice mixture.
  6. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot.
  7. Simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until the rice is cooked and all the liquid is absorbed.
  8. Remove the pot from heat and let it sit for a few minutes.
  9. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.
